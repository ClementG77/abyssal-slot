# Abyssal — How the Game Works

A plain-language guide to the **Abyssal** slot. No technical knowledge needed — this
explains everything: the board, the symbols, how you win, the special feature, and the
six ways to play.

---

## The idea in one paragraph

Abyssal is a deep-sea slot where you sink into the abyss to wake a colossal **Leviathan**.
Symbols drop onto a **6×5 board** (6 columns, 5 rows). You win whenever **8 or more of the
same symbol** appear *anywhere* on the board — they don't need to be in a line. Winning
symbols vanish and new ones fall in to fill the gaps (a **tumble**), which can chain into
more wins from a single spin. The star of the show is **The Eye** — the Leviathan's single
glowing eye — which can multiply your winnings dramatically.

---

## How a single spin plays out

1. **The board fills** with symbols (6 columns × 5 rows = 30 symbols).
2. **Wins are checked:** if any symbol appears **8+ times**, that's a win.
3. **Winning symbols explode** and disappear.
4. **Symbols above fall down**, and new symbols drop in from the top to refill the board.
5. **Wins are checked again.** If there's another win, it repeats. Each winning drop is
   called a **tumble** (or cascade).
6. When a drop produces no more wins, the spin ends and your total is paid.

> One board can have **several different winning symbols at once**, and a single spin can
> chain **multiple tumbles** in a row — that's where the big wins come from.

## The Eye — the heart of the game ⭐

This is what makes Abyssal special. Two things work together: the **Gaze** and **The Eye**.

### The Gaze (a charge meter)
Every time a spin produces a winning tumble, the **Gaze** charges up by **1** (you can think
of it as "how many times the board paid in a row this spin"). A long chain of tumbles builds
a big Gaze.

### The Eye
Sometimes a single **Eye** appears on the board. When it does, it has:
- a **type** — either **ADD** or **MULTIPLY**, and
- a **starting number** — one of 2, 5, 10, 25, 50, or 100.

At the **end of the spin**, if you won *and* an Eye is present, the Eye combines its starting
number with your Gaze to make one big **multiplier**, which is applied to everything you won
that spin:

- **ADD Eye:** multiplier = starting number **+** Gaze
- **MULTIPLY Eye:** multiplier = starting number **×** Gaze

> **Example.** Your tumbles won **2× your bet**, and you built a Gaze of **3**.
> - With an **ADD** Eye starting at 10 → multiplier = 10 + 3 = **13** → you win **26× your bet**.
> - With a **MULTIPLY** Eye starting at 10 → multiplier = 10 × 3 = **30** → you win **60× your bet**.

**ADD** is the common, friendly version. **MULTIPLY** is rare and explosive — it's what drives
the biggest wins.

### The near-miss
Most winning spins **build a Gaze but no Eye appears** — and then the Gaze is simply lost
(you just keep your normal win). That tension — *"will the Eye show up this time?"* — is part
of the fun. When the Eye *does* land on a charged-up spin, it feels huge.

## Free Spins (the bonus round)

- **How you trigger it:** land **4 or more Conch (Scatter)** symbols. In the base game,
  scatters can **land as the board tumbles** — so you might have 2 or 3 and watch another one
  drop in to trigger the bonus (Sweet-Bonanza style). 3 scatters is a tense near-miss.
- **Scatters pay instant cash too:** when you trigger, the number of scatters pays a bonus
  straight away — **4 = 3×, 5 = 5×, 6 = 100×** your bet (paid on top of the spin, not affected
  by the Eye). So more scatters = more instant money.
- **What you get:** a **flat 15 free spins** every time, no matter the scatter count.
- **The Snowball (like Gates of Olympus):** during Free Spins there's a **banked multiplier**
  that starts at ×1 and **carries across the whole feature**, paying off on the spins where an
  Eye lands. The two Eye types grow it very differently:
  - **ADD Eye (common):** adds `(your Gaze + its number)` to the banked multiplier — a gentle climb.
  - **MULTIPLY Eye (rare & prized):** **multiplies your Gaze** by its number, and adds that to
    the banked multiplier. A long winning chain (big Gaze) plus a MUL Eye adds a big chunk —
    so MUL Eyes are the ones you chase.
  A spin with **no Eye just pays its normal win** (the banked multiplier waits for the next
  Eye), and the banked multiplier only ever **grows** over the feature.
- **Retrigger:** land 3+ scatters again during the feature to win **+5 more spins**.
- A feature can run up to **30 spins**.

## The six ways to play

| Mode | Cost | What it is |
|------|------|-----------|
| **Base** | 1× bet | The standard game. The Eye is rare; mostly the friendly ADD type. |
| **Ante** | 1.25× bet | Same game, but pay 25% more for **more frequent Eyes and scatters** (the bonus triggers more often). |
| **Buy Free Spins** | 100× bet | Skip the wait — **buy the Free Spins** feature directly. |
| **Super Spins** | 20× bet | **One single spin, with the Eye guaranteed.** A quick, punchy one-shot — no free-spins round, just one charged-up spin. |
| **Super Bonus** | 500× bet | A supercharged Free Spins: the Gaze charges **twice as fast** and the explosive MULTIPLY Eye is **common**. The mode that most often chases the max win. |
| **Ultimate** | 300× bet | **One spin with several Eyes at once**, which combine (add and multiply together). A high-stakes gamble: most spins win little (a net loss), but a few big Eyes can pay enormously — up to the 15,000× max. Huge or nothing. |

> **Super Spins** is deliberately simple: you buy it, you get **one spin** (guaranteed Eye),
> you see the result. No bonus round, no counter — just one big shot.

## Quick recap

- Drop symbols → **8+ of a kind anywhere wins** → winners explode and **tumble** for more.
- A winning chain charges the **Gaze**.
- If **The Eye** appears, it turns your Gaze into a **big multiplier** (ADD = gentle,
  MULTIPLY = huge) on your whole spin.
- **4+ Conch scatters** (which can drop in mid-tumble) **pay instant cash** (4 = 3×, 5 = 5×,
  6 = 100×) **and** start **Free Spins** (a flat 15 spins), where a **banked multiplier** grows
  each time an Eye lands and pays off big on those Eye spins (Gates-of-Olympus style).
- Six modes let you play it safe (Base), boost the action (Ante), or buy straight into the
  features (Buy Free Spins, Super Spins, Super Bonus, Ultimate).
- Top win **15,000× your bet**; **96% RTP** throughout, with a continuous win spread all the
  way up to the cap on every mode.
